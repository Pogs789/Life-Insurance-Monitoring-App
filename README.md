# Life-Insurance-Monitoring-App
A mobile app that aims to automate the recording of life insurance payments availed by insurance holders, and automate the computation of insurance dues that must be remitted by insurance agents.

## Problem Statement
Insurance Agents sometimes struggle to monitor this month's insurance payments being made by the insurer that were needed to be remitted to their respective insurance agency when it comes to manually computing all payment dues as well as identifying whose insurance holder has their payment due in this month.

## Features of the App
- User Management - Managing of users that use the app from: insurance agents, system administrators,
- Insurance Policy Management - Managing of the Insurer's Insurance Policy a client can avail.
- Payment Rremittance Monitoring - Making payment remittances to the Insurance Companies made easy by automating the computation of payments as well as easily monitor each insurance premium that is due.
